I am passionate about the power of Goals!

The "Smart Goals" strategy is the most effective way to create goals

  1. Productivity
    • I love goals because they help us to be productive.
    • The productivity is not just busy work, but is work that has a bigger purpose.
    • Daily life becomes more purposeful and meaningful
  2. Progress
    • I love goals because they help us change and progress.
    • Setting goals implies changing from our natural habits or self.
    • This change is what helps us to progress!
  3. Change the World!
    • I love goals because GOALS CHANGE THE WORLD!
    • Setting goals leads to changing lives and gives us willpower to do hard things!
    • As we change the world for the better, we find that goals bring JOY!
